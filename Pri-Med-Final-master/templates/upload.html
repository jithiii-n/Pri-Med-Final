Pneumonia Screening

Pneumonia is a significant global health concern, causing millions of hospitalizations and fatalities each year. Early detection through chest X-rays can greatly enhance patient outcomes by enabling prompt medical intervention. Our AI-powered system analyzes uploaded X-ray images to identify potential signs of pneumonia, providing a preliminary assessment to assist healthcare professionals in timely diagnosis and treatment.

Oral Cancer Detection
